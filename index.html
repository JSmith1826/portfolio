    College Hockey Statistics Project

    A comprehensive data analysis project centered around college hockey, leveraging Python for web scraping, data cleaning, and sophisticated analysis of historical and current season statistics. This project dissects the performance metrics, player production rates per capita, and strategic game decisions like empty net plays across various NCAA seasons.

    Utilizing advanced statistical models and visualization techniques, the project offers unique insights into the dynamics of college hockey, including adjusted game scores and time spent leading or trailing in games. Detailed Jupyter notebooks document the methodologies used for data collection, transformation, and analysis, culminating in interactive visualizations that highlight trends and patterns within the sport.

    The ultimate aim is to provide fans, analysts, and enthusiasts with a deeper understanding of the factors that influence college hockey outcomes and player development. This ongoing work showcases not just the analytical potential within sports data but also the rich history and evolving strategies of college hockey.

    DataSource: CollegeHockeyNews.com

    Baseball Park Project

    An ongoing machine learning project to evaluate baseball fields using satellite mapping, spatial data , weather data and game results in an effort to understand which parameters play large rolls in the outcome of baseball games

    So far the project that plots over 1000 baseball parks ranging from youth fields to major league and international stadiums. A future goal is to use AI to better identify and plot the locations and dimentions of baseball parks using publically available satellite

    In addition to 10 years of Michigan high school baseball results data from over 12,000 NCAA Division 1 college baseball games was scraped from ESPN.com and matched to geographic location with historical weather data in order to create a dataset that can be used to train a machine learning model to predict the outcome of baseball games.
