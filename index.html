About Me

I'm a tech enthusiast who thrives on variety, adaptability, and learning. My path has taken me through the dynamic world of hospitality, the demanding field of logistics, and the creative space of startups. In each role, I found myself drawn to the unique challenges and intricate details that others often overlooked. From managing bustling kitchens and discerning the quality of artisanal charcuterie and cheeses to helping launch a canoe and kayak livery, and even dabbling in the ever-evolving field of medical cannabis - my journey has been anything but ordinary. One consistent thread has been my passion for solving problems, often with a technological slant. Whether routing delivery vehicles, designing websites, or managing social media, I've always found a way to incorporate my love for tech. Recently, I embarked on a more formal tech journey by completing a six-month Data Analytics Bootcamp. This intensive training has equipped me with skills in Python, JavaScript, and Tableau, adding depth to my natural curiosity and problem-solving abilities. Whether it's cleaning up data, creating engaging visualizations, or mapping data onto Google Earth or OpenStreetMap, I've honed the ability to transform complex data into actionable insights. I'm excited to leverage this potent combination of diverse experience and technical acumen to tackle your business challenges. Let's connect and create something extraordinary together!
